# FAQ for the PUSH LAP app ## Why is the new race not displayed in the app? New races (including individual sessions as part of a race weekend) are added up to 30 minutes befeore the scheduled start as this is when the official data provider starts emitting data. We appreciate your patience. ## Are new features planned for the app? Yes, this is only a first iteration of the app, more exciting features are expected to come to the app in the near future. So stay tuned for that! ## How can I make suggestions about the app? Feel free to reach out to the dev team at formulatelemetry@gmail.com if you have any suggestions.
